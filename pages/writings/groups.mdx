### Every cyclic group is abelian.

Every subgroup of a cyclic group is cyclic.

Let $G$ be a cyclic group of order $n$ and suppose that a is a generator for $G$. Then $ak = e$ if and only if $n$ divides $k$.

$$
\kappa(n) = n^{n - 2} \text{ for the complete graph $K_n$.}
$$
